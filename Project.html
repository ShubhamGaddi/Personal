Courses, Trainings & Internships

Projects Done



  • Adjustable Heel Design using Bamboo – B. Tech 4th Semester Project [May 2019]
    • Built a heel attachment that could be adjusted to 2 different heights using different types of bamboo only.
    • Project was made for a course and was capable of operating at the two heights of 3inches and 5 inches.

  • Line follower with Obstacle Detection [April 2019]
    • Built a line follower bot with obstacle detection and retracking using Arduino Uno.
    • Bot worked perfectly and components used include DC motors, motor – driver IC, Arduino UNO, breadboard, wires and other small things as per requirements.

  • Genesis [March 2019]
    • Part of the combustion ATV building team of the college that participated in MAC [Mega ATV Championship] 2019.
    • Was a part of the chassis team and worked actively in suspension and transmission teams as well.
    • The vehicle built was able to clear the technical inspection, electrical as well as the braking test at the competition held in Goa in March 2019 and ran continuously for a total time of approximately 4.5 hours during the endurance testing.

  • E-Genesis [February 2019]
    • Part of the electrical ATV building team of the college that participated in ESI [Enduro Student India] 2019.
    • Was a part of the chassis team and worked actively in suspension and transmission teams as well.
    • The vehicle built was able to clear the technical inspection as well as the electrical tests at the competition held in Pune in February 2019.

  • Digital Tachometer – B. Tech 3rd Semester Project [November 2018]
    • Built a tachometer that could give the speed of a rotating object in RPS in the form of a digital readout. Project was made for a course.
    • Components used include basic logic gates, decade counters, IR couple, 7 – segment displays, breadboards and wires.
    • Worked perfectly and gave readings with an accuracy of approximately 85%.
